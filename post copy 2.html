Everyone has their own hobbies, right? I have one too. I used to be very skinny and always felt depressed and sick. But after starting to work out, my mind became healthier. There is a saying that goes "a sound mind in a sound body," and I believe in that.

Working out not only provides physical training but also mental training. When you do intensive workouts, you learn how to endure suffering and be patient. Moreover, building a solid body takes time, so working out is a course in learning diligence and dedication. I'm going to continue working on it. Thanks!
