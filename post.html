Junghyun Yoon

Welcome to my space! This website serves as a platform for me to practice my coding skills and to remind myself of my life's goals. It brings me great pleasure to have you here, and I am grateful for your kind attention.

I hail from the tough streets of Incheon, South Korea, which was notorious for its gang activity. As a young boy, I was part of a group of friends who, due to our unfortunate circumstances, turned to bullying. Some of them even ended up dropping out of school or getting arrested for their ill-advised criminal actions. However, my life took a drastic turn when I discovered Jesus Christ, and since then, my dream has been to spread God's love in whatever way possible.

I pursued my bachelor's and master's degrees in theology from a seminary school in both Korea and the United States. However, my aspirations now extend beyond the realm of the church. I am eager to explore other industries and put my heart and philosophy into everything I do.

I am young and have boundless ambition. I aspire to achieve greater heights and push beyond the limitations of my comfort zone.
