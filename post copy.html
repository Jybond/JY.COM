My purpose and goals
d
Junghyun Yoon

In this post, I want to share my beliefs, vision, and specific goals. I believe that a dream without purpose is meaningless, and a dream without goals is just a dream. In my opinion, my purpose is to love God and love my neighbor, and by neighbor, I mean everyone in the world. So, I want to carry these values of love throughout my life journey.

I have a grand vision of building a community that transcends borders and promotes philanthropy. Whether through a business venture or an NGO group, my goal is to generate revenue that can be utilized for charitable purposes. In this way, I hope to make a positive impact on the world and leave a lasting legacy.

Now, let me share my specific goals for this vision. In three years, I am going to be ordained as a reverend, and at the same time, I will be a good programmer. So, three years later, I will be a full-time programmer and part-time pastor, allowing me to work outside of the church and in society. After building my career, I plan to release some apps that I can use for commercial purposes while creating an app that can help people and connect them to all the resources they need to donate.

When I turn 40, if I have enough money, I want to build a church or temple where people can stay for their spiritual journey. Additionally, I want to use that building to help people in need, especially those who are struggling with drug addiction. To do that, I will need to make a lot of money, and I think investing in real estate is crucial, so I am going to work on that too.
